About Us

Why Healthify?
Healthify helps you secure a wealthier future by taking care of your health. We help patients to access specialist doctors where they can consult a doctor and get an online prescription. We believe patients' fundamental right is to get world-class patient services at their doorstep. We provide compassionate healthcare services to our patients because they don't need additional hassle if they are sick. We want our patients to rest and recover from their illnesses, and we will take care of their healthcare needs. Healthify is on a mission to make healthcare more patient-centric. We are reducing the cost of care by optimizing resources using technology, increasing patient satisfaction by providing quality care with a one-stop solution, and Minimizing stress by reducing time and travel.
Why are we Different? Healthify founders and Doctors want to achieve 100% accurate diagnosis before starting a patient care plan. To achieve that, we took our technology innovation one step forward; we implemented a system that integrates with Biosensors, AI algorithms, and IoT devices to record required patient vitals on our platform for accurate diagnostics. We also understand that early diagnosis is required to treat patients efficiently and provide patients with a fighting chance to overcome fatal illnesses.
